What is a Heliosphere

The heliosphere, in short is the magnetosphere and the outermost atmospheric layer of the Sun. It takes the shape of a huge bubble-shaped body in space.

The heliosphere is continuously "inflated" due to continuous exposure to plasma originating from the Sun called Solar Wind.
